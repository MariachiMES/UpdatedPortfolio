My name is David Ortiz. I have made my living in case management for over 10 years in various facets of the industry; homelessness, veterans affairs, patient advocacy, immigration and mental health. My great passion in life has been my music; my most recent endeavors being Mariachi Entertainmen System and Studio Comal, creative outlets for my musicianship and editing skills. I have recently taken on the task of learning front-end development with the hopes of building a career that will bring me personal satisfaction a sense of stabiliy. My first opportunity to work in the games industry came when I received a commission from Riot Games to create a mariachi version of an existing League of Legends song "The Curse of The Sad Mummy". I was able to continue my work in the games industry by working on original music for DrinkBox Studios' Guacamelee! 2 as well as working on mariachi covers of existing music from the franchise also featured in-game. My most recent work on games is on an independent steam game called "Town of Zoz", and action-adventure RPG about a boy and his connection to his indigenous culture through food.
